About Us

Bees tend to work in an orderly society where collaboration is key. Here at The Colony, that is no different.

The Colony is a web-design consultancy team based in Manchester, made up of 5 unique and talented individuals, each with strengths in a number of different areas of web-design. Whether it is a project from the very start or a specific stage of web-design, we can help.

Below are the members of our team:

Nathan Kerr

Nathan is the developer in our team, and his speciality is focusing on website layout and CSS. He has worked on projects in Barclays such as CloudIt Documents in the past, and in his spare time enjoys playing football and keeping fit.

Olly Wheelton

Olly is the tester in the team, and specialises in testing the websites built by the developer to ensure they work as they should. Olly has experience working on Barclays Blue Rewards, and in his spare time he plays cricket and supports Manchester United.

Zach Widdowson

Zach is another of the testers in our team, and he specialises in testing front end design to ensure it all flows correctly. Zach has worked on Project Bicep in the past, and is also fully qualified as a football referee.

Megan Thomason

Meg is the User Experience member in our team, who specialises in turning the ideas from the user research into a realistic design. Meg has previous experience designing user experience projects, and in her free time enjoys listening to a wide variety of music.

Camilla Griffiths

Camilla is our project manager at The Colony, and has been involved in numerous web development projects such as MyBarclays Open Banking. Outside of project management Camilla likes to travel the world.
